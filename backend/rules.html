Quick, business-friendly rules

  1. Bet window: Place a bet within the 5s timer shown on the home page. Bets after the timer are ignored.
  2. Payouts: If the wheel lands on a money segment and you have a valid stake, you get the displayed reward multiplied by your stake tier (see code!).
  3. House edge: The platform keeps a configurable edge (demo: 30%). This preserves long-term sustainability.
  4. Responsible play: This is a demo. Do not use real money without a proper regulated backend and audit.

Legal-ish note (simple)

SpinX is a demo & prototype. Real payouts require verified identity, KYC, and a compliant payments provider (M-PESA integration is supported in design but not active in demo files).
